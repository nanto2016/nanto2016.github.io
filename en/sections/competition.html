Description of the CRC competition

This year, CRC Robotics has a new version of their competition: TakTik 2025.

This competition will be from February 19, 2025, to February 22, 2025. It's In Québec 9955 avenue Papineau, Montréal.

The goal of this competition is to give a chance to young people in high school and CEGEP to make a robot which will play in multiple heats, allowing the team to show their skills in various domains.

Game

The game is the most important componant of the competition. It allows participants to show their skill through strategy and teamwork.

Considering the importance of this section, the game will have its own section because a simple description can't summarize it all.

Robot design

The robot design is an essential component to the robot operation because it creates the mechanisms of the robot like the wheels.

This component has its own section which gives a detailed explanation of how we made our robot.

In summary, the robot componant allows all teams to participate in the game of this year and create the essential concepts to the adequate robot operation.

Robot construction

The robot's construction is another essential component of the robot. It allows the robot to move and perform its functions.

The CRCduino controls the motors and other electronic components, which makes the robot's mechanisms work. Regarding the programming, more challenges will be organized by the CRC, in python, making up the programming component.

Kiosk

The kiosk isn't an obligatory section for the game of this year. It allows schools the possibility to have space to show their work to other teams. This space should be safe and it should be able to show the robot to the public.

To conclude, the kiosk is a place where the school's team shows their theme in a safe public place for all.

Video

The video consists of explaining a part of the game or the CRC. The video can have a narrative that is textual sequence, fictive or journalistic. The video can be a documentary.

In summary, the video consists to describe the CRC competition or an aspect of the game.

We were unable to make a video this year.

Tutorial

The tutorial is where the teams make a video explaining how the electronic current works and explain one of the building steps of the robot's complex mechanisms.

In brief, the tutorial allows for better explanations of the school's robot.

We were unable to make a tutorial this year.

Web site

The website isn't a necessary component of the competition. It is what you are currently reading. It documents of all the important components of the competition and outlines the team's journey.

To finish, the website is the place where all the important information of the competition is found along with the school resources.
